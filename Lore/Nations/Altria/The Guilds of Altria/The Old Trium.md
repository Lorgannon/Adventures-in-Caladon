# House Ashford: Lords of Law
Leader: Elora Ashford
Ashford is largely composed of high elves who emigrated from the Northern Lands as they were invaded by the Empire. They are devoted to order and rule of law above all else. Despite some mild elitism, they are fair and tend to act in the best interest of the people. True Lordships of this house are rewarded on the basis of blood, but honorary, landless titles are granted to efficient bureaucrats under their employ.

House Baelish: Lords of War
Leader: Bratar Maddrum
Harsh but fair, House Baelish is the oldest house in Altria. They trace their blood lines to Grey Orc immigrants from the old borders of [[The Kalbrimbian Wastes]]. Owing mostly to their militaristic tradition, House Baelish was always less based on blood than on honor, and as such, handled the transition to popular rule quite well. All high-ranking lords and ladies within the house began as simple recruits, and were granted those titles after completing acts of bravery on or off of the battlefield 

House Caplan: Lords of Trade
Leader: Midon Brownlock
of the original three houses, House Caplan was one of the most enthusiastic about the transition to guildstate. leadership of House Caplan is decided purely by wealth which means it tends to be the least honorable of the triumvirate. Cutthroat dealings and targeted attacks among the various powers in the house are not an uncommon occurrence. This house has deep ties to Oswia.
