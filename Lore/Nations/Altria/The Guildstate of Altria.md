The Guildstate of Altria is one of the most cosmopolitan countries in Caledon. Founded by noble houses from every corner of the known world, Altria is almost entirely defined by its acceptance of immigrants and refugees from the surrounding regions. This willingness to assimilate and accept other cultures has allowed Altrian innovations, both magical and technological, to vastly outpace their neighbors. However, these innovations tend to be less focused than either of Altria’s neighbors. With [[The Tsaibaki Empire|Tsaibaki]] to the North investing solely in arcane weapons of war, and [[The Commercial Confederation of Oswia|Oswia]] to the west emphasizing exclusively industrial innovation. 

Despite it's founding as an aristocracy, Altria has demonstrated a pronounced ability to adapt to the needs of its citizenry; and as the desire for representation and egalitarianism grew, the ruling houses of Altria ceded power to the people, allowing commoners to enter into the places of power previously reserved for nobles, and permitting for the creation and ascendency of new, upstart houses (at this point more similar to trade guilds or unions) as the need arises.
