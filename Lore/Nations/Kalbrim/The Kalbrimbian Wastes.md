Eons ago, when the world was young, the great nation of Kalbrim ruled the western lands of Caladon. Little is known of the particulars of their culture or government, as those finer details were lost to time and disaster. What is known, or at least speculated, is that their arcane abilities far out matched those of modern nations. Myths tell of great floating cities, sorcerers with the power to move mountains, and rituals that allowed their casters to remain alive for centuries. Artifacts recovered from the region seem to confirm these stories; and magic items pulled from the ruins of the old world fetch a high price on the black market. 

But expeditions to discover these relics are not without their hardships. Where Kalbrim once stood there is only shattered wasteland, infested with fiends and monstrosities of all shapes and sizes. The event that rendered this once great kingdom to a sun-blasted desert is known as "The First Incursion" and is largely believed to be the event that brought demons and extra-planar beings to Caladon originally.
